enum EleWise.ELMA.CAB.Events.PublicationScope

Defines the scope for a publication of an T:EleWise.ELMA.CAB.Events.EventTopic.

Enumerator
Global 

Indicates that the topic should be fired on all the F:EleWise.ELMA.CAB.Events.PublicationScope.WorkItem instances, regarding where the publication firing occurred.

WorkItem 

Indicates that the topic should be fired only in the F:EleWise.ELMA.CAB.Events.PublicationScope.WorkItem instance where the publication firing occurred.

Descendants 

Indicates that the topic should be fired in the F:EleWise.ELMA.CAB.Events.PublicationScope.WorkItem instance where the publication firing occurred, and in all the F:EleWise.ELMA.CAB.Events.PublicationScope.WorkItem descendants.
